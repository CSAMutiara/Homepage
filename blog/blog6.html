sixth blog entry [ date: 10 . 6 . 17 ]

more info about alerts

Alerts:

The website is alerting you that you have succeeded at a task!
The website is alerting you of some useful information!
The website is warning you of something that can happen if you proceed!
The website is alerting you of the dangers of proceeding with the task at hand!

Alert formatting:

Did you know?

Alerts can have headings! They can also have links. Look at the examples below. (Also, don't forget that alerts require contextual classes)


Well done! You've made it this far. Click here to continue to the next step.
Heads up! You have some missing user info. Click here to update your profile.
Warning! You've made a lot of changes. Save your progress so you don't lose any of your hard work.
Oh no! You've entered an incorrect password. Enter it again correctly, or you can request a new one.
